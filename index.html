Privacy Policy

Notch — Last updated: May 2026

Short & honest version

What is Notch?

Notch is a shared-living app that helps flatmates manage chores, split expenses, and keep a shared shopping list. Your data stays within your household group.

What data we collect

  • Account info: your email address and display name (you set this yourself).
  • Household data: chores you create or complete, expenses you log, shopping items, and leaderboard points. This is shared only with members of your household.
  • Device token: an anonymous push notification token so we can send you in-app alerts (e.g. when a housemate completes a chore). This is not tied to your identity.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not run ads.

Where your data lives

All data is stored on Supabase — a GDPR-compliant infrastructure provider hosted in the EU. Your data never leaves EU servers.

Who can see your data

Only the members of your household can see your household's chores, expenses, and shopping list. No one outside your household — including us — can read your household data.

Push notifications

We use Expo's push notification service to deliver alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the app.

Deleting your data

You can leave your household at any time from Settings → Leave household. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email us at support@notch.app and we will process your request within 30 days.

Children

Notch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the date at the top of this page and notify users via the app.

Contact
