Delete Your Notch Account

Notch — by Airwinn — Last updated: June 2026

Account & data deletion

Delete your account in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Notch app.
  2. Go to the YOU tab → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Or request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email support@notch.app from the email address linked to your account with the subject “Delete my account”. We will process the request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email address and display name).
  • Your profile data: points, streaks, and leaderboard history.
  • Chores, expenses, and shopping items you created.
  • Your push notification token.

What may be kept, and for how long

  • Shared household records: expenses you were part of may remain visible to your former household (with your identity removed) so their balances stay correct.
  • Subscription records: Apple, Google, and our subscription provider (RevenueCat) retain purchase records under their own policies; we never hold your card details.

No other data is retained after deletion.

Questions
